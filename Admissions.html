Admissions

Begin your journey with Infinity University and apply today to our world-class programs.

How to Apply

At Infinity University, the admissions process is designed to ensure that students have the right tools and support to succeed. Follow the steps below to apply:

  1. Choose your program of interest.
  2. Review the admission requirements for your selected program.
  3. Submit your online application through our portal.
  4. Upload the required documents (transcripts, letters of recommendation, etc.).
  5. Complete the admission interview (if applicable).

Important Dates

Mark your calendar with these important dates:

  • Application Deadline: November 1, 2024
  • Admission Decisions: December 15, 2024
  • Program Start Date: January 10, 2025

Admission Requirements

Applicants are required to meet the following criteria to be eligible for admission:

  • Completion of a recognized secondary education or equivalent qualification.
  • Proficiency in English (TOEFL/IELTS scores if applicable).
  • Strong academic performance in prior education.
  • Personal statement outlining goals and motivations.
  • Optional: Letters of recommendation from previous instructors or employers.
